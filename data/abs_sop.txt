SECTION: PURPOSE
To provide a standardized procedure for posting questions in Keeper, handling client communication, performing allocations, reconciliations, and payroll tasks to support accurate and timely month-end close.

SECTION: SCOPE
This SOP applies to all Moneypenny team members involved in financial review, bookkeeping, and client communication activities in Keeper and related platforms. It ensures efficient workflow, accountability, and consistency in handling client files.

SECTION: PREREQUISITES
Access to platforms: Keeper, Dropbox, Gusto, Slack, Outlook, Financial Cents.
Familiarity with client-specific folders and tools, especially for Movement Medical.
Understand internal conventions for categorizing transactions, vendor naming, and communication protocols.
Weekly allocations and reconciliations must be completed as scheduled.

SECTION: RESPONSIBILITIES
TEAM LEADER: Maintains accuracy and updates SOPs during admin hours. Responsible for reviewing the workflow, assigning tasks, ensuring timely completion, and conducting a final review before submission to the client.
PREPARERS: Review the workflow for new tasks and consult with the Team Leader before starting. Follow up on previous communications and perform a self-review of tasks before submitting them to the Team Leader.

SECTION: ALLOCATION WEEKLY
Analyze P&L to determine entry type: Cash vs Accrual.
For Accrual Basis: Record bill or adjust payment only with supporting documents.
Check Dropbox for documents.
Follow category review rules: Meals use Meals vendor. Travel use Travel vendor. Fuel use Fuel vendor. Auto use Auto vendor.
Do not create new vendor names.
Add customer names to deposits if available.
Match payroll transactions before accepting.
Threshold: Use sub-accounts if expense category is greater than $2,500 or multiple expense types exist.

SECTION: RECONCILIATION PROCESS
Retrieve statements from Dropbox or request from Rebecca in Keeper.
Ensure there is no opening balance discrepancy.
All expenses must have payee names and checks must have numbers.
Uncleared transactions must be validated or matched.
Do not void transactions without client confirmation.
All reconciled transactions must belong to the current period.

SECTION: PAYROLL GUSTO
Download Payroll Reports from Gusto.
Manually sync JE if not auto-imported.
Match Payroll JE with gross pay and taxes in bank feed.
Match Gusto report balances.
Payroll clearing account must be zeroed.
If payroll clearing account is not zeroed: Create a report of pending amounts or deductions per employee.
Review Uncleared Gusto Transactions monthly in FC.
Prepare Excel summary for gross wages and taxes such as Movement Medical Payroll 2024 xlsx.

SECTION: REVIEW PROFIT AND LOSS
Check for abnormal or negative balances.
Check for capitalizable expenses.
Check for consistency in recurring transactions.
Check for proper coding for Bank Charges, Merchant Fees, and Payroll Fees.
Check that salary and tax matches to payroll reports.

SECTION: REVIEW BALANCE SHEET
Match bank and CC account balances to statements.
Verify depreciation JEs.
Zero out payroll liability accounts or reclassify.
Confirm loan interest segregation.
Validate sales tax payments and equity account classification.

Post queries in Keeper under relevant transactions and accounts.

Complete all MP Team sub-tasks.
Once reviewed, set Keeper status to Ready for Rebecca.

SECTION: W2 MATCH PROCESS
Verify W-2s and 1099s between Gusto and QBO.
Log into Gusto and QBO and download relevant reports.
Make sure to match Wages and Taxes for 2024 before working on matching W2.
Select Download W-2 and W3s. Select 2024 and download.
Download Payroll summary report by Employee for the year.
Location: Gusto then Switch company then File Name then Gusto home page then Reports then Payroll Journal legacy.
Choose Summary Only.
Select all options except Employee Earnings Breakdown and Reimbursements.
Click Generate Report.
Steps to match W2:
Step 1: Create Excel using previous year format. Location: Dropbox then All files then ABS Team folder then select the file then 2025 then W2 Excel.
Step 2: Compare with Employee order in Gusto Payroll summary report. Delete employees who are no longer active and add new employees for the current year. Ensure employee order in Excel matches Gusto report.
Step 3: Compare the W2 of each employee with annual Gusto Payroll report to update excel.
Update Excel W-2 sheet in two sections: Employee Gross Wages split by designation such as Administration, Officers, Sales Commissions, Management and Operations Departments. Employee Taxes: update employee tax amounts excluding insurance amounts while comparing W-2 and Gusto Payroll reports.
If there is any difference between W-2 and Gusto: clearly mention the reason in the remarks column.
In the Movement Medical file, an extra column is available for Benefits and 401k amounts Box 12. Update this column only with the employee portion as per the W-2 report. Box 12 in W-2 includes both employee and employer amounts.

SECTION: 1099 CONTRACTOR PAYMENT
Search each file on the Gusto Home page by switching between files to confirm whether contractor payments are available before proceeding.
Download the annual Contractor Payment Report from both QBO and Gusto to compare and reconcile contractor payments.
Contractor Payment Report from Gusto: Log in to Gusto, switch to the required file, click Contractor Payments on the Gusto home page, select the options and generate the report.
Contractor Payment Report from QBO: Log in to QBO, switch to the required company file, click Reports from left-side menu, select Standard Reports, click Profit and Loss, set the reporting period to Last Year, select Contractor under Cost of Goods sold if applicable, export the Contractor report in CSV format.
Prepare Contractor Payment Sheet:
Create the Excel file using the same format as per 2024 reference.
Add contractor names in accordance with both the QBO Contractor Report and the Gusto Contractor Report.
Update the 1099 Earnings column using data from the Gusto Contractor Report.
Update the As per QBO Contractor column by linking it to the corresponding data in the QBO Contractor Sheet.
Match and validate all details using 2024 Excel reference.
After completing this report, save the report in ABS TEAM FOLDER in Dropbox.
Updated 01/31/2026.
